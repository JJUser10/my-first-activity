Workouts

Description: A push-up is a bodyweight exercise that strengthens your chest, shoulders, and core.

Steps:

  1. Start in a plank position with hands shoulder-width apart.
  2. Lower your body until your chest almost touches the floor.
  3. Push back up to the starting position.
  4. Repeat for the desired number of reps.

Description: A squat is a strength exercise that involves bending the knees and hips to lower the body as if sitting in a chair.

Steps:

  1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Lower your hips down and back as if sitting on a chair.
  3. Keep your chest up and knees behind your toes.
  4. Return to standing position and repeat.

Description: A plank is a core strengthening exercise that involves holding a position similar to a push-up. Planks can help you build strength, support your spine, and prevent injury.

Steps:

  1. Start in a forearm plank position.
  2. Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels.
  3. Engage your core and hold the position.
  4. Maintain for the desired duration.
