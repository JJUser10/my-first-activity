Vincenzo 2021

IMDb

Genre: Dramas, Korean, Comedies, Social Issue

Why I choose this i shows different actions like in terms of video and also in the acting of every actors, that creates an wonderful results. He is a lawyer, who works for the Mafia as a consigliere. She is the type of attorney who will do anything to win a case. Vincenzo Cassano falls in love with her. He also achieves social justice by his own way.
